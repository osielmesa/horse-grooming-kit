## Description

The Dandy Brush is the workhorse of the set. With its stiff natural bristles and beechwood handle, it is designed to remove heavy dirt, dried mud, and loose hair from the horse's body. It is typically the first brush used during a grooming session. Its deeper bristle profile and heavier build reflect its role as the most intensive cleaning tool in the kit.

## Function & Use

- First-stage grooming tool
- Removes caked mud, heavy dirt, sweat residue, and loose hair
- Used with firm, flicking strokes to lift debris away from the coat
- Not recommended for sensitive areas (face, legs) — use the face brush there
- Suitable for body, neck, hindquarters, and legs
