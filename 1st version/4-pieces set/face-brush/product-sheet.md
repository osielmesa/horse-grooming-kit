## Description

The Face Brush is the smallest and most delicate brush in the set, specifically designed for use on the horse's sensitive facial areas: around the eyes, ears, muzzle, and forelock. Its compact size and very soft natural bristles allow precise, gentle grooming without causing discomfort to the horse in areas where larger brushes cannot safely reach.

- Removing fine dust and debris from the face
- Safe for use on the most sensitive areas of the horse
- Compact handle gives greater control and precision
