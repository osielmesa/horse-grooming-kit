## Description

The Handcrafted Leather Bag is the storage and presentation unit of the 4-piece set. It houses all four grooming tools and serves as the primary packaging the customer sees and receives the product in. Beyond storage, it is a premium accessory in its own right — lightweight, portable, and elegant enough for use at shows or competitions. It positions the set as a luxury gift item.
