> **Correction from initial documentation:** This item was first listed as "Tail Comb." Official Amazon product photos confirm it is a **pin brush** — metal pins on a rubber cushion pad, with a long beechwood handle. Documentation updated accordingly.

---

## Description

The Mane & Tail Pin Brush is the longest tool in the set. Unlike the three oval body brushes that use a leather hand strap, this tool has a classic long paddle-style beechwood handle with a hanging hole at the end. Metal pins are set into a rubber cushion pad on the oval head. The "MS / MASIEL SPARK" logo is laser-engraved on the back of the handle. It is designed to detangle and smooth the mane and tail without breaking the hair.

## Function & Use

- Primary tool for detangling and smoothing the mane and tail
- Metal pins glide through long hair without snagging
- Rubber cushion pad absorbs pressure to minimize hair breakage
- Suitable for applying detangling sprays or conditioners through the tail
- Long handle provides leverage for working through thick, knotted tails
- Can be used to lay the mane flat after washing
