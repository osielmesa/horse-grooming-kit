## Description

The Curry Comb has a distinctive **multi-ring coil design**: several concentric circular serrated metal rings of decreasing diameter, stacked and mounted on a **turned beechwood handle** with the MS logo laser-engraved. A central screw/bolt holds the rings together. The serrated rings rotate against the coat to loosen dirt and shed hair. This is a premium version of the traditional coil curry comb. Exclusive to the 8-piece set.

- Turned/lathe-shaped beechwood handle, light natural finish

## Function & Use

- Loosening compacted dirt, mud, and dander deep in the coat
- Removing shedding hair during seasonal coat change
- Used with circular or short back-and-forth strokes
- Used before body brushes in the grooming sequence
- Not recommended for use on legs, face, or bony areas
- Can also be used to clean the Dandy Brush by combing through the bristles
