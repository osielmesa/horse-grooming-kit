## Description

The Dandy Brush in the 8-piece kit is an oval body brush with a light beechwood body and a dark brown leather hand strap fixed with two brass rivets. The bristles are cream/natural colored and appear long and moderately stiff. It retains the oval-with-strap format seen in the 4-piece body brushes, with natural light beechwood (not dark stained as in the 4-piece version).

## Function & Use

- First-stage grooming tool for removing heavy dirt and dried mud
- Used with firm flicking strokes to lift debris off the coat
- Not recommended for face or sensitive areas — use Massage Brush for those
- Suitable for body, neck, hindquarters, and legs
