## Description

The Grooming Sponge is the lightest tool in the 8-piece set. It is used for bathing, applying grooming products (shampoo, coat conditioner, fly spray), and cleaning sensitive areas such as around the eyes, nostrils, and dock. It is exclusive to the 8-piece kit and not included in the 4-piece set.

## Function & Use

- Applying shampoo and coat conditioner during bathing
- Rinsing and wiping sensitive areas (eyes, nostrils, dock, sheath)
- Applying fly spray or insect repellent evenly across the coat
- Cleaning wounds or irritated skin areas
- Wiping down the horse after light exercise
- Applying hoof oil or dressing around the coronet band
