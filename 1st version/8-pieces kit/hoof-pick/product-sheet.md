# Hoof Pick (with Brush)

![Hoof Pick](https://m.media-amazon.com/images/I/41net-bexpL._SL1196_.jpg)

**Set:** Masiel Spark Premium Horse Grooming Kit (8 Pieces)  
**ASIN (Set):** B0GBZJVY8B

---

## Description

The Hoof Pick has an ergonomic **contoured beechwood handle** with a hanging hole at the base and the "MS" logo laser-engraved on the face. A metal hook pick is set at the top of the handle. A stiff natural bristle brush is mounted alongside the pick head, used to sweep out loosened debris after picking. The handle has a wave/contour shape that fits the palm naturally. Exclusive to the 8-piece kit.
