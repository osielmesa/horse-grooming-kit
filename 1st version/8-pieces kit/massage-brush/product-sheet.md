## Description

The Massage Brush has a **beechwood body with dark wooden cylindrical nubs/pegs** — not rubber as originally assumed. It is held in the hand via a dark brown leather strap riveted with two brass rivets. The nubs are rounded and protrude from the flat face of the wooden body. It is used to massage and stimulate blood circulation, loosen deep-seated dirt, and distribute natural coat oils. The compact palm-fit design gives precise control.

## Visual Details (from product photos + Real Product Photos — Google Drive, May 2026)

- Compact oval beechwood body, **light natural beechwood finish** (confirmed from real photo — light tan/natural color, NOT dark like 4-piece brushes)
- **Dark brown cylindrical wooden nubs** arranged in rows on the brush face — nubs are a darker wood than the body for visual contrast
- **Dark brown/chocolate smooth leather hand strap** with **2 large dome-head brass rivets** — rivets are prominently visible and larger than those on the 4-piece brushes
- "MS" logo laser-engraved on the body
- No bristles — entirely wood nubs (wood-on-wood massage tool)
