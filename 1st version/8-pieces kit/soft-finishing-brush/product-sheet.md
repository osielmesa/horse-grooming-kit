## Description

The Soft Finishing Brush in the 8-piece kit has a distinctly different design from the 4-piece version. Instead of an oval body with a leather hand strap, it features a **rectangular block-shaped beechwood body** with no hand strap — held directly in the palm. The bristles are uniformly white and very soft. The "MS" logo is laser-engraved on the side face of the wooden block. It works alongside the Dandy Brush and Massage Brush to complete a full grooming sequence.

- Rectangular block-shaped body (not oval like 4-piece version)

- Natural light beechwood color — no dark stain
- "MS" logo laser-engraved on the long side face of the block
- No leather hand strap — palm-grip design
- Rounded corners on the wooden block

## Function & Use

- Final-stage coat polishing after the Dandy Brush and Massage Brush

- Brings out shine on coat, neck, and hindquarters
- Soft enough for daily use and sensitive skin areas
