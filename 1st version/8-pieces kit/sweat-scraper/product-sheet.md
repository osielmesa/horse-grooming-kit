## Description

The Sweat Scraper has a premium design: a wide crescent-shaped **gold/brass-finished metal blade** with a black rubber edge trim for safe contact with the horse's coat, mounted on a **turned beechwood handle** with the MS logo laser-engraved. The blade is held to the handle via a brass Y-shaped bracket. It is the most visually distinctive tool in the kit. Exclusive to the 8-piece set.

## Visual Details (from product photos + Real Product Photos — Google Drive, May 2026)

- Wide crescent-shaped blade, **gold/brass — highly polished, mirror-like finish** (confirmed from real photos)
- **Black rubber edge trim** along the outer curved scraping edge (confirmed)
- Blade face has **3 small brass rivets** visible — appear to secure the rubber edge strip
- Blade attached to handle via a **brass Y-shaped bracket** (confirmed — warm gold metal)
- Turned/lathe-shaped beechwood handle, **light natural beechwood finish** — same light wood as other 8-piece tools (NOT dark like 4-piece brushes)
- "MS" logo laser-engraved on the handle (confirmed from real photo — visible on turned section of handle)
- The handle has a slightly bulbous/torpedo-turned shape for grip
